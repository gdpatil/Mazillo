01

Product Overview

Mazillo is the operations platform for Navtek Marine Services. It runs the full commercial lifecycle of a marine service or supply job — from the first customer enquiry, through quotation, order execution and sub-vendor purchasing, to tax invoicing, payment tracking and a live profit-and-loss balance sheet.

Every record is connected: a quotation knows its enquiry, an order knows its quote, an invoice knows its order, and the balance sheet rolls those links into order-wise margins. The same chain feeds the reporting suite and the coordinator commission engine, so a single data entry never has to be repeated downstream.

03

Core Operational Flow

A single thread runs from enquiry to balance sheet. Each stage hands its data to the next; two derived processes — service reporting and commission — branch off the order; and every stage feeds the reporting layer.

04

Workflow Stages in Detail

STAGE 1Enquiry

A new case is opened the moment a customer makes contact. The Case ID is auto-generated; the rest captures who, what and where, plus the coordinator who owns the case.

• Auto Case ID + region (IND / SG)
• Registered customer company & vessel
• Job location / port & job scope
• Amount quoted to customer (INR / USD)
• Quote received / sent dates
• Owning coordinator / employee
• Vendor reference quotes
• Status & remarks

STAGE 2Quotation

A quotation is built against a chosen case, inheriting its customer, vessel and location. Line items carry quantity and rate; a discount and GST are applied, and the finished quote exports to a branded PDF.

• Pick a case or edit a saved quote
• Auto quotation number (QTN)
• Line items — qty × rate
• Discount (flat / percent)
• GST computation
• Export to PDF
STAGE 3Order

When a quote is confirmed the case becomes an order — the execution hub. It records the customer PO, the real costs of delivery, and any sub-vendor purchase orders, then drives invoicing and the order's profit.

• Auto order number (ORD)
• Customer PO reference
• Costs & expenses
• Sub-vendor purchase orders (PO)
• Customer payments received
• Generate tax invoice / proforma
• Coordinator commission (computed)
• Close or cancel the order
One tax invoice and one proforma are permitted per order — re-opening either returns to the existing document rather than duplicating it.
STAGE 4Invoice

Invoices are raised from an order (or a quote). Two document types share one engine: the final Tax Invoice and an advance Proforma. GST (CGST / SGST / IGST), discount and currency all flow through, and payments are tracked to a received / pending balance.

Tax Invoice — INV Proforma — PINV
• CGST / SGST / IGST handling
• Discount (flat / percent)
• Payments & pending balance
• Print / export the invoice
05

Service Reports

A branch off the order, the service report is the engineer's field record of work carried out on a vessel. It documents the equipment, the fault, the work done, time on the job and customer satisfaction — and links back to its order.

• Auto SR number, date & vessel / port / IMO
• Equipment, make / model, serial number
• Fault reported & engineer's report
• Materials used & spares removed
• Time log — working, waiting, workshop, travel
• Job completed / follow-up flags
• Telephone calls & transport
• Customer satisfaction rating

Balance Sheet

The balance sheet is the financial endpoint of the flow. For each order it computes profitability directly from the linked invoices and recorded costs.

• Order-wise profit & loss
• Invoice ledger with payment status
• Awaiting / partially / fully received
• INR & USD totals side by side
• Cancelled records excluded from all totals
• Dedicated Excel export
08

User Management & Roles

Users are created in Settings with a name, email, password and role. The role decides which modules appear in the navigation — access is enforced at sign-in. Users can be disabled (kept for history) rather than deleted.

Data backup & restore. Settings → User Management exports the entire dataset to a JSON file and restores it on another browser — the reliable way to move or archive data offline.
09

Commission System

Coordinators are rewarded for converting enquiries into orders. One rule, set in Settings, applies to every non-cancelled order and rolls up per coordinator on the Coordinators page. Each enquiry that becomes an order counts as one conversion.

The Coordinators page surfaces the rollup: Total Commission Payable, Orders Converted and Active Coordinators, a per-coordinator performance table (orders handled, on hold, basis, commission) and an order-by-order commission detail. Coordinators see only their own figures; managers and admins see everyone.

10

Numbering, Currency & Lifecycle

Document numbering
<PREFIX>-<REGION>-MM-YYYY-NNN

The running number increments per prefix + region + month, following today's date.

QTN · Quotation ORD · Order PO · Sub-vendor PO INV · Tax Invoice PINV · Proforma
Multi-currency

Amounts are held in INR or USD. Totals, reports and the balance sheet present each currency on its own line so the two are never mixed.

Cancel, don't delete

Orders, invoices and POs are cancelled, not deleted. They turn grey, keep their number so the sequence is never reused, and drop out of every total, report and the balance sheet.
